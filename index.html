I'm a Toronto-based marketing professional with 5+ years of experience turning cultural moments into brand movements. My work lives at the intersection of organic social media, brand strategy, and experiential marketing, where community-centered storytelling drives real, measurable results.

Through my freelance agency, Red Thread, I've partnered with brands across Toronto's creative ecosystem, from independent studios and coffee shops to large-scale events drawing thousands. Whether it's a campaign that goes viral on TikTok overnight or an event activation that captures the city's attention, I build strategies that resonate.
